what's new in the school

  • School Canteen to provide healthy food

    The school canteen is now fully aware about the effects of junk food on kids' health. Now it is decided that children will no longer be served foods such as vadapav or cold drinks. Instead they will be served with healthier options such as sandwiches and fruit juices which do more good.

  • School to now send attendance tracking messages to parents

    It has now been decided that after the attendance, a note saying that the student arrived and is attending the class will be sent to the parents. This service will work for all grades i.e from I to XII. Beware!
